# DT Growth Hacker Assignment - Profitability Analysis

This project contains a comprehensive profitability analysis workbook that examines different business scenarios for a product sold on Amazon. The analysis explores how various factors impact net profit and break-even points.

## Project Overview

The `Task_1.xlsx` workbook contains 5 different analytical scenarios:

### Step 1: Base Case
- **Purpose**: Explores the default scenario using standard parameters
- **Key Metrics**: Standard SP, COGS, Units Sold, Fixed Costs, Interest, Tax, and CAC
- **Analysis Focus**: Baseline profitability assessment

### Step 2: Increased CAC
- **Purpose**: Shows the impact on profit when Customer Acquisition Cost increases
- **Key Changes**: Reduced units sold (4000 vs 5000) to simulate higher acquisition costs
- **Analysis Focus**: CAC impact on overall profitability

### Step 3: Reduced Selling Price
- **Purpose**: Demonstrates how lowering the selling price affects margins and profitability
- **Key Changes**: Increased fixed costs (15000 vs 10000) to simulate market pressures
- **Analysis Focus**: Price sensitivity and margin analysis

### Step 4: Increased Units Sold
- **Purpose**: Explores the effect of selling higher units on net profit and break-even CAC
- **Key Changes**: Higher selling price (35 vs 30) to drive increased volume
- **Analysis Focus**: Scale economics and volume impact

### Step 5: Increased Tax Rate
- **Purpose**: Analyzes the impact of higher tax rates on profitability
- **Key Changes**: Increased tax rate (25% vs 20%)
- **Analysis Focus**: Tax optimization and after-tax profitability

## Key Parameters Analyzed

Each scenario examines these critical business metrics:
- **Selling Price (SP)**: Product selling price on Amazon
- **COGS**: Cost of Goods Sold
- **Amazon Fee %**: Platform commission (15%)
- **CAC**: Customer Acquisition Cost (variable)
- **Units Sold**: Monthly/periodic sales volume
- **Fixed Costs**: Operational overhead
- **Interest**: Financial costs
- **Tax Rate**: Corporate tax rate
